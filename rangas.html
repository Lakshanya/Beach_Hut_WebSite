About

A relaxed base on Sri Lanka's surf coast

Arugam Bay is famous for the waves that draw surfers from around the world — but it's also close to national parks and sites of cultural heritage, making it a base for more than just a surf trip. Beach Hut sits right in front of the sand, close to the bay's main attractions.

  • Homely cabanas and a genuinely friendly, familiar staff
  • Recognised by The Guardian, UK for budget travel in Sri Lanka
  • Walking distance to Arugam Bay's surf breaks and town centre
  • Well suited to backpackers, surfers, and longer-stay travellers

Restaurant

Home-style plates, ocean view

An open, relaxed dining spot where the day's surf report is the main conversation. Local Sri Lankan dishes alongside easy travellers' favourites, served at a pace built for a beach town.

Meals are served through the day for guests and walk-ins — a natural meeting point after a morning session in the water.
